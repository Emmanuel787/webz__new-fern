About

Prior to focusing on heart centered consciousness, Fernmarie accumulated over 15 years of corporate experience in fortune 500 enterprises including Microsoft, Skype, Boeing, Lockheed Martin, and NASA. She holds a B.Sci in Physics and a M.Sci in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Puerto Rico. Later acquired a M.Sci in Human Centered Design from the University of Washington.

As a hobby, Fernmarei spent 12 years with Zero Gravity Corp., helping to open space travel to civilians, and later in 2017, she became a World Champion dancer.
